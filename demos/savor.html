Our Philosophy

Origins of Flavor

Born from the rich tapestry of Trinidadian culture, Savor represents the convergence of East Indian spices, African roots, and modern gastronomy.

Our kitchen is strictly Halal certified, ensuring that every guest can indulge without compromise. We source our cocoa from the Gran Couva hills and our catch from the waters of Manzanilla.
